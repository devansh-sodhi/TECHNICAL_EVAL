# Rebuttal Coaching Scenarios

## Marcus — T-1042

**Customer Threat Quote:** > "This is terrible service. I'm going to report you."

### Role-Play Scenario

**Simulated Customer Message:** I'm going to take this to your manager if you don't fix this now!

**Ideal Agent Response:** I understand that you're feeling frustrated, and I truly apologize for the inconvenience this has caused you. I want to help you as much as I can. Let me see if I can escalate this issue to someone who can assist you further.

**Coaching Goal:** To improve the agent's ability to de-escalate tense situations and demonstrate empathy towards the customer's feelings.

**Technique Demonstrated:** Active listening and empathy.
